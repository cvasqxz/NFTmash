welcome to NFTmash

nftmash is a game designed to create a community-driven nft ranking system based on subjective beauty, using the same idea of facemash to choose the coolest nft from a random pair

the gameplay is simple, you have to pick your favorite from 2 random nfts in only 30 seconds, then we add 1 point to the nft your pick
